Full English Breakfast

Full English Breakfasts are a popular England delicacy.This morning meal is conveniently offered throughout the day. The breakfast consists of bacon, fried eggs, sausages, baked beans, mushrooms, tomatoes and toast accompanied with tea or coffee.

Market Eats

The food markets in London offers a great selection of street food and fresh produces. The diversity in the city of London creates a great melting pots of different cuisines at the food markets. Borough market, Berwick street market, Brixton Village and Camden market are some of the must-visit food markets in London.

Pubs

Experience the British hospitality sitting at a bar, drinking and snacking on some chips. Pubs are the most authetic way to experience the drinking culture of London. Some of the most popular Pubs include: The George Inn, The Mayflower, The Ten Bells, Lamb and Flag, The Churchill Arms and many more.st-visit food markets in London.

Afternoon Tea

The British tradition of Afternoon Tea is best enjoyed when served with stack of sandwiches, scones and cakes. Served in patterned bone china and porcelain teaware, this activity is a very eye appealing and royal experience to share with a group of friends. The Afternoon tea served at The Ritz, Harrods, The Savoy and The Lanesborough are some of the crowd favorites.
